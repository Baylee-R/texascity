Things to do in Texas City


NAME: ADDRESS: ACTIVITIES:
Summer Rodeo 527 Rodeo Rd, Texas City, IA Rodeos and Youth Rodeo events held every Friday through Sunday from June through August. Scheduled events throughout the year. Cancellations subject to weather due to arenas being outdoor. Click to see some clips from the 2019 summer rodeo series!!
Rural Roots Ranch 411 Ranch Dr, Texas City, IA Milk cows, meet and be educated about farm animals, and enjoy trail rides on the rural side of Texas City to go back to our Texas cowboy roots. Corn maze and pumpkin patch during the fall. Haunted barns in October.
Military Demonstrations 1 Cav Cr, Texas City, IA As a military base is nearby, there are several types of military demos that visitors and citizens can attend for free. The cavalry division puts on demonstrations every first and third Friday of each month at 10:00am. Military Police K9 demos are put on every second Thursday at 9:00am.
Biking/Running/Walking Trails Several locations throughout the city There are many trails both in and around the city to walk, run, or bike on. However, you must bring your own bike. Trails are closed October 15, and reopened on April 5.
YMCA 1613 Sandlewood Rd, Texas City, IA Many activities, which include but are not limited to: swimming, rock climbing, basketball, yoga, running (indoor track, treadmills), zumba, etc.
Billy's Blacksmithing 249 Mosey St, Texas City, IA Anyone can come over to Billy's Blacksmithing and learn to smith their own tools. Classes on horseshoes, pliers, screwdrivers, and tongs are taught daily.
