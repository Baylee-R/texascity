Directions to Texas City



Texas City is a larger city located in the center of Iowa making it easy to find. Use your GPS, a road map or Google Maps to visit us! Texas City is about 2 hours away from the border to Missouri. We are located 30 minutes north of Des Moines.

The advantage of being in the middle of the state is the easy access to all of the other popular locations in Iowa, including major cities on the borders. This allows us to engage with fellow Iowans and appreciate their cities. Our location also allows us to share our magnificent city easier with fellow Iowans. Our location allows us to have a community with all of Iowa! We would love for you to join our community, visit us soon!

The distance from select major cities from Texas City:

Sioux City, driving distance from Texas City: 198 miles
Cedar Rapids, driving distance from Texas City: 127 miles
Davenport, driving distance from Texas City: 167 miles
Council Bluffs, driving distance from Texas City: 128 miles
