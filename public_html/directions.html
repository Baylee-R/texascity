Directions to Texas City


Since Texas City in located in the center of Iowa and it is a big city for Iowa it is not that hard to find. Really all you have to do is type into your GPS Texas City, Iowa or you can look in a map to look for roads to get there! Texas City is about 2 hours away from the border to Missouri.

The advantage of being in the middle of the state is the easy access to all of the other popular locations in Iowa, including major cities on the borders. The distance from select major cities from Texas City is listed below.

Sioux City, driving distance from Texas City: 198 miles
Cedar Rapids, driving distance from Texas City: 127 miles
Devenport, driving distance from Texas City: 167 miles
Council Bluffs, driving distance from Texas City: 128 miles
